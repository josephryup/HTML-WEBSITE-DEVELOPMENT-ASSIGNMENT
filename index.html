Substance Classifications

Class A Substances

Highly addictive controlled drugs including Heroin, Cocaine, and LSD. Possession leads to maximum penalties under the 1993 2021 Narcotics Act.

Class B Substances

Controlled medicinal drugs like Morphine and Pethidine, subject to strict prescription monitoring.
